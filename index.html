Growth and Decay

Definition

Growth and decay describe the processes by which quantities either increase or decrease over time. These phenomena are often represented using first-order differential equations, which link the rate of change of a quantity to the quantity itself.

P(t) = P0ekt

  • P(t) = population at time t
  • P0 = initial population
  • e = the base of the natural logarithm (~2.718)
  • k = growth or decay rate
  • t = time

Definition
